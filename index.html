Species and Subspecies


There are two species of gorilla: the Eastern gorilla and the Western gorilla. Eastern gorillas are found in some places, and Western gorillas are found in others. Within each of these two species there are two subspecies. The subspecies of the Eastern gorilla are the Eastern lowland gorilla and Mountain gorilla. The subspecies of the Western gorilla are the Cross River gorilla and Western Lowland gorilla.
